Troubleshooting

  • AI coach not responding: check your internet connection, wait a moment, then try sending the message again.
  • Microphone not working: open iPhone Settings, find Vocality, and make sure Microphone and Speech Recognition are enabled.
  • Presentation simulator not starting: make sure Camera and Microphone access are enabled for Vocality.
  • Image upload issues: allow Photo Library access and try a clear image under normal file size limits.
  • File analysis issues: text-based PDFs and plain text files work best. Scanned PDFs may not contain readable text.
  • Habit data missing: habits are stored locally on your device, so deleting the app may remove local progress.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 1, 2026

Last Updated: June 1, 2026

Vocality ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the Vocality mobile application (the "App") processes when you use AI speech coaching, presentation simulation, voice recording, image upload, file upload, habit tracking, onboarding, settings, and related features.

Vocality does not require users to create an account. The App is designed to help users practice speaking and presentations, not to sell data, show ads, track users across apps or websites, or build advertising profiles.

App Store Privacy Summary

For App Store privacy labels, Vocality may collect Photos or Videos, Audio Data, and Other User Content when users choose to use features that require those data types.

These data types are used for App Functionality only. They are not used for third-party advertising, developer advertising or marketing, analytics, product personalization, or tracking.

Vocality does not intentionally link submitted photos, audio, transcripts, prompts, or files to a user account, because the App does not require accounts. Vocality does not use these data types to track users across other apps or websites.

Information We Process

  • Typed messages and prompts: when you type into the AI Speech Coach, your message is sent to Vocality's backend so the App can generate an AI response.
  • Speech, audio, and transcripts: if you use voice recording, speech coaching, or presentation analysis features, the App may access your microphone and Apple speech recognition services to record or transcribe speech, calculate speaking metrics, and provide feedback.
  • Photos, images, and files: if you choose to upload an image, PDF, text file, or other supported file, the App may process that content. Images and extracted file text may be sent to Vocality's backend and AI service providers so the App can analyze the material and respond to your request.
  • Camera-based presentation feedback: if you use the presentation simulator, the App may access your camera while the feature is active to provide real-time practice feedback such as posture or eye-contact-related guidance. Vocality does not intentionally save or upload full camera video from the simulator.
  • Local habit and preference data: habits, streaks, theme settings, onboarding status, and similar preferences may be stored locally on your device.
  • Limited technical request data: hosting and service providers may process limited technical data such as request metadata, IP address, timestamps, and error information as needed to deliver, secure, maintain, and troubleshoot the service.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide core App functionality, including AI speech coaching, presentation feedback, transcripts, speaking metrics, image and file analysis, and practical improvement suggestions.

We use speech and transcript information to calculate metrics such as word count, words per minute, filler-word count, filler words per minute, and presentation feedback. We use local habit and preference data to save your habits, streaks, app theme, onboarding status, and related app settings on your device.

AI Processing and Service Providers

Vocality uses a backend service hosted through Render to process AI requests without placing the OpenAI API key directly inside the App.

Content you submit for AI features, including typed messages, transcripts, uploaded images, extracted file text, and related prompts, may be transmitted to OpenAI to generate responses and analysis.

The App may also use Apple services and frameworks, including microphone access, camera access, Photos, Files, and Speech Recognition. Apple may process certain information according to Apple's own privacy practices.

These providers process information to provide App functionality. Vocality does not authorize them to use submitted content for third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.

What We Do Not Do

  • Vocality does not sell your personal information.
  • Vocality does not show third-party ads.
  • Vocality does not use submitted speech, transcripts, photos, files, or prompts for third-party advertising, developer advertising or marketing, or cross-app tracking.
  • Vocality does not require an account, collect phone numbers, collect contacts, collect payment card information, collect precise location, or provide unrestricted web browsing.
  • Vocality is not a medical, legal, financial, or emergency service.

Data Storage and Retention

Some information, such as habits, streaks, app theme, and onboarding status, is stored locally on your device. This data remains on your device until you delete it, reset it, or delete the App.

Audio recordings used for voice features may be temporary and used to create transcripts or feedback. Transcripts, prompts, uploaded images, and extracted file text may be transmitted off-device when needed to provide AI features.

Vocality does not intentionally store submitted AI prompts, transcripts, images, or file contents longer than reasonably necessary to provide the requested feature, operate the service, troubleshoot errors, maintain security, or comply with legal obligations. Third-party providers, including OpenAI and Render, may process and retain data according to their own policies and service terms.

Your Choices and Controls

You can control access to microphone, speech recognition, camera, photos, and files through your device settings. If you deny a permission, the related feature may not work properly.

You can choose not to use AI features, voice recording, presentation simulation, image upload, or file upload at any time. You can delete locally stored habits, streaks, app preferences, and onboarding data by deleting the App from your device.

Because Vocality does not currently provide user accounts, we may not be able to identify or retrieve past AI requests unless you provide enough information for us to locate a specific support issue. You may contact us with privacy questions or data-related concerns.

Children's Privacy

Vocality is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information processed by the App and avoid exposing sensitive credentials in the mobile application. However, no method of transmission or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

International Processing

Depending on where you use the App and where service providers operate, information may be processed in countries other than your own. These countries may have privacy laws that differ from those in your location.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes take effect means you accept the updated Privacy Policy.
