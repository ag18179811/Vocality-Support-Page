Troubleshooting

  • AI coach not responding: check your internet connection, wait a moment, then try sending the message again.
  • Microphone not working: open iPhone Settings, find Vocality, and make sure Microphone and Speech Recognition are enabled.
  • Presentation simulator not starting: make sure Camera and Microphone access are enabled for Vocality.
  • Image upload issues: allow Photo Library access and try a clear image under normal file size limits.
  • File analysis issues: text-based PDFs and plain text files work best. Scanned PDFs may not contain readable text.
  • Habit data missing: habits are stored locally on your device, so deleting the app may remove local progress.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 1, 2026

Vocality respects your privacy. This policy explains how the app collects, uses, processes, and shares information when you use speech coaching, presentation simulation, habit tracking, onboarding, image upload, file upload, and related features.

Information we may process: typed messages, speech recordings, transcripts, camera-based presentation feedback, uploaded images, uploaded files, extracted file text, habits, streaks, theme settings, onboarding status, and limited technical request data needed to operate the app.

How information is used: to provide AI speech coaching, generate feedback, calculate speaking metrics, analyze presentation practice, support uploads, save local app preferences, improve reliability, and respond to support requests.

AI processing: content you submit for AI features, including prompts, transcripts, uploaded images, and extracted file text, may be sent to our backend and OpenAI to generate responses and analysis.

Local storage: habits, streaks, app theme, and onboarding status may be stored locally on your device. Deleting the app may remove this local data.

Permissions: you can control Microphone, Speech Recognition, Camera, Photos, and Files permissions in your device settings. Some features may not work if a required permission is disabled.

No sale of personal information: Vocality does not sell your personal information and does not use submitted content for third-party advertising.

Children: Vocality is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Security: we take reasonable steps to protect information processed by the app, but no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Contact: if you have questions about privacy or support, email anandg.articulacyapp@gmail.com.
